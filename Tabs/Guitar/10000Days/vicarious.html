Adam doesn't use clean channels EVER. He always has his amps running a heavy 
crunch, to get any clean tones he rolls his volume down on his bridge pickup 
and picks lightly. Similarly, he brings his guitar volume back up to bring in distortion.

Effects: delay, and mxr micro amp for boosting the level of certain parts.
