Ok guys, I was ast the Tool concert in Peoria, with binoculars, and finnally 
figured out the main riff. Its not a 3p2p0 pulloff.
What you have to do is kind of drag your finger down the top two strings to 
get the triplet sound. It's kinda tricky at first,
but I swear to god that this is correct.


Effects: Apparently, according to a bunch of interviews, he used the foxx 
tone machine for this song.
In addition to that he uses delay, Wah, and a talkbox.
