Before I start I'd like to say that there are different ways to play
this opening, as there are several layers of slide guitar. But here is my
interpretation of one way to play it, using a live video of a performance
of the song as my semi-source for my tab. Take it as a simple guide, not an exact
science.

Some other points to know:

1. It starts off quite faint

2. Keep your slide on your pinky

3. This is a monsterous beast, best of luck to everyone and take your time
