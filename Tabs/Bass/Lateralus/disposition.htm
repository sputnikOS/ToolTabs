I will have 3 new symbols for this tab.
When you see a note with  ^ after it, it means the note is struck and the whammy is rocked forward.
When you see a note with  * after it, it means the note is struck and the whammy is rocked SLOWLY Back.
When you see a note with ** after it, it means the note is struck and the whammy is QUICKLY rocked fwd then back.
                                                                (This is what gives the “weeooh weeooh” sound..lol)
