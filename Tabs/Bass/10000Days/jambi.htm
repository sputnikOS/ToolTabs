Note- It is important to know Justin’s setup before you attempt this song. No amateur bassist will

be able to pull this song off sounding like JC unless you have 2 amps. Throughout the song Justin is

always running both amps. One is running clean with his effects pedals and another back in the mix running

in the back with gain/dirty setting either using his ProCo TurboRat with an EQ..

The distortion you hear in the beginning is a stomp box, but even when it is

off for other parts of the song you will hear the grit throughout the song. Good luck! =)

 

Another small note is that Justin’s flanger and delay are next to each other on the pedal board making it easy

for him to trigger them both simultaneously. Good luck playing this song if your board is any different. =)

you will hear a "woooo" noise on the album. That is done by Justin quickly engaging the 
sutowah on and off for that particular note. He does this live as well and it is very audible.
