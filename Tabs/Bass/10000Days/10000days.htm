(5:39-6:23)  Justin uses a raking technique here. The part is the middle of the riff 
he keeps the 5th fret g fretted with his index and his ring finger on the 7th fret d. 
You quickly pluck (or rake) up with your index (or middle) plucking finger through the g and d strings 
so that you are not quite playing a chord but the two notes in sequence quickly. This is a tricky riff. Lots of 
practice!
